HDFC ERGO OPTIMA SECURE HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY - POLICY WORDINGS

1. Coverage Benefits
(a) In-patient Treatment: Covers medical expenses for hospitalization exceeding 24 hours.
(b) Pre-hospitalization: Medical expenses incurred up to 60 days prior to admission.
(c) Post-hospitalization: Medical expenses incurred up to 180 days after discharge.
(d) Day Care Procedures: All day care treatments are covered.

2. Secure Benefit
(a) The Base Sum Insured is automatically doubled instantly upon purchase of the policy.
(b) If you buy a ₹10 Lakh policy, your coverage becomes ₹20 Lakh from Day 1.

3. Exclusions (What is NOT covered)
(a) Investigation & Evaluation: Expenses related purely to diagnostics with no active treatment.
(b) Obesity/Weight Control: Surgical treatment for obesity is excluded unless medically necessary.
(c) Cosmetic Surgery: Plastic surgery or aesthetic treatments are excluded.
(d) Hazardous Sports: Injuries from skydiving, scuba diving, etc.

4. Waiting Periods
(a) Initial Waiting Period: 30 days for all illnesses (except accidents).
(b) Specific Illnesses: 24 months waiting period for stones, cataracts, hernia, etc.
(c) Pre-existing Diseases: Covered after 36 months of continuous coverage.

5. Claims Procedure
(a) Cashless: Intimate the insurer 48 hours before planned hospitalization or within 24 hours of emergency admission.
(b) Reimbursement: Submit claim documents within 15 days of discharge.
