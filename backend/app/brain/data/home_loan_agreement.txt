HOME LOAN AGREEMENT - STANDARD TERMS AND CONDITIONS

1. Definitions
"Borrower" means the person(s) named in the Schedule.
"Lender" means ArthMitra Finance Ltd.
"Floating Rate" means the interest rate linked to the Repo Rate published by RBI.

2. Interest Rate Reset
(a) The interest rate is subject to reset every 3 months based on the prevailing Repo Rate.
(b) The spread over repo rate is fixed for the tenure of the loan, unless there is a deterioration in the credit score of the borrower.

3. Prepayment and Foreclosure
(a) Floating Rate Loans: No prepayment penalty or foreclosure charges shall be levied on floating rate home loans sanctioned to individual borrowers, as per RBI guidelines.
(b) Fixed Rate Loans: A prepayment penalty of 2% of the principal outstanding will be charged if the loan is closed within 12 months using funds from another bank (takeover). No penalty if closed from own sources.

4. Default and Consequences
(a) An event of default occurs if the EMI is not paid within 90 days of the due date (NPA classification).
(b) In case of default, the Lender has the right to enforce security interest under the SARFAESI Act, 2002, and take possession of the mortgaged property without court intervention.
(c) Default interest of 2% per month (24% per annum) will be charged on the overdue amount.
