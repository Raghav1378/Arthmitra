SBI BLUECHIP FUND - SCHEME INFORMATION DOCUMENT (SID)

1. Investment Objective
To provide investors with opportunities for long-term growth in capital through an active management of investments in a diversified basket of large cap equity stocks.

2. Risk Profile (Riskometer)
This product is suitable for investors who are seeking:
- Long term capital appreciation.
- Investment in equity and equity related instruments of large-sized companies.
Risk Level: VERY HIGH.

3. Fees and Expenses
(a) Entry Load: Note applicable.
(b) Exit Load:
    - 1% if redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment.
    - Nil if redeemed after 1 year.
(c) Total Expense Ratio (TER): Up to 2.25% of daily net assets.

4. Tax Implications (Equity Oriented Scheme)
(a) Short Term Capital Gains (STCG): If sold within 1 year, taxed at 15%.
(b) Long Term Capital Gains (LTCG): If sold after 1 year, taxed at 10% on gains exceeding ₹1 Lakh.

5. SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)
- Minimum SIP Amount: ₹500 per month.
- Minimum Tenure: 6 months.
- Step-up Facility: Available to increase SIP amount annually.
