RESERVE BANK OF INDIA
MASTER DIRECTION - CREDIT CARD AND DEBIT CARD ISSUANCE AND CONDUCT DIRECTIONS, 2022

1. Billing and Payment
(a) No hidden charges shall be levied on credit cards. All charges must be explicitly mentioned in the Most Important Terms and Conditions (MITC).
(b) Card issuers shall ensure that the wrong bills are not raised and issued to customers. In case a customer protests any bill, the card issuer shall provide explanation and, if necessary, documentary evidence.
(c) Card issuers do not follow a standard billing cycle for all credit cards issued. In order to provide flexibility in this regard, cardholders shall be provided a one-time option to modify the billing cycle of the credit card to their convenience.

2. Recovery of Dues
(a) Card issuers shall ensure that their recovery agents strictly adhere to the Fair Practices Code for Lenders.
(b) Card issuers/their agents shall not resort to intimidation or harassment of any kind, either verbal or physical, against any person in their debt collection efforts, including acts intended to humiliate publicly or intrude upon the privacy of the credit card holders' family members, referees and friends, sending inappropriate messages either on mobile or through social media, making threatening and/or anonymous calls, persistently calling the cardholder and/or making calls which would be perceived as harassment.
(c) Recovery agents are allowed to call borrowers only between 08:00 hours and 19:00 hours.

3. Closure of Credit Card
(a) Any request for closure of a credit card shall be honoured within seven working days by the credit card-issuer, subject to payment of all dues by the cardholder.
